# **App Name**: HIRELS

## Core Features:

- Dual-Role Authentication: Secure sign-up for Clients and Professionals with specialized forms for contact details, gender, age, and location parameters.
- Location-Based Pro Search: Find professional service providers like tutors or plumbers specifically by matching professional work types and local PIN codes.
- Secure Document Hosting: Enables Professionals to upload up to two high-quality skill certificates to their profiles for credibility and verification.
- AI Service Descriptor Tool: A tool that uses generative AI to help professionals write compelling descriptions and categorized tags for their service listings based on their provided skill certificates.
- WhatsApp Lead Bridge: One-click connection button on professional profiles to initiate WhatsApp conversations for instant service booking.
- Interactive User Dashboard: Unified dashboard for clients to manage saved searches and for professionals to toggle their availability and service rates.
- Global/Local Professional Directory: Centralized database management system to handle hundreds of categories of skilled workers with location-sensitive indexing.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color is a Trustworthy Royal Blue (#2F61D2), evoking professionalism and security. The background is a clean, barely-there tint of the primary color (#F1F4FA).
- The accent color is a Vibrant Cyan (#2CB3CC), providing high contrast for search buttons and important CTA interactions.
- Headline font is 'Space Grotesk' for a modern, tech-forward directory feel; body text is 'Inter' to ensure maximum legibility for detailed professional bios.
- A grid-based directory layout emphasizing profile cards that clearly display service charge, location, and verified certificates at a glance.
- Minimalist, thin-line stroke icons used to represent various professional categories like education, maintenance, and finance.
- Smooth slide-in transitions between the role selection screen (Client vs. Professional) and the multi-step registration forms.
